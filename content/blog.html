How to Download YouTube Videos Legally

Downloading YouTube videos can be a grey area. While streaming is always permitted, downloading videos without permission violates YouTube’s terms. However, there are exceptions: if the video is under a Creative Commons license, or if you’re downloading your own content, it’s allowed. Always respect copyright and use downloaders responsibly.
